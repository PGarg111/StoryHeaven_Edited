Digital Library

Welcome to your free digital library! In this library you will find all the new and popular books for middle and high schoolers. The website is divided using the different genres. All you have to do is click on the genre you want to read and then navigate through the page looking for which book you want to read. Or search for your book through the search bar on the home page! Use the filters to find what genre books you are interested in. Once you find a book you want to read, click on the book cover and it will take you to a page where you can read the book. If the book cover is transparent, it means the book isn't available yet. Enjoy your stay!
